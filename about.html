About The Artists

Vincent Rinehart

Artist & Co-Founder

Vincent Rinehart has been painting since the 1970's. His work explores and conveys the look, feel and mood of human experiences through an Expressionistic and Visionary approach.

Selected three times for the Metro Montage at the Marietta Cobb Museum of Art, Georgia. Exhibited at Roswell Visual Arts Center (2022-2023). Featured at Atlanta Contemporary Art Center (2023). Cincinnati Art Club selected his painting "Guidance" for a juried exhibit in Sept., 2023. The Charleston Museum accepted the painting "Charleston" for their collection in 2023.

In May 2024, Rinehart with Karl Kroeppler held an exhibition at the Roswell Visual Arts Center. Rinehart, along with Kroeppler and Dana Gioia, former Chairman of the National Endowment for the Arts, were interviewed by WABE-NPR "City Lights" in April 2024. In April 2025, Rinehart exhibited several paintings in a showing at THE ART PLACE in Marietta, GA.

Rinehart graduated from Cal State Long Beach with a BA in Business Admin. and a minor in Visual Art.

Website: www.vincentrinehartartist.com

Karl Kroeppler

Artist & Co-Founder

Kroeppler received his MFA in Drawing and Painting and BA in Studio Art from Arizona State University. He exhibits and lectures in local and national venues.

He exhibits and lectures in local and national venues including Joan Derryberry Gallery, Tennessee Tech University (Solo Exhibition), Spiva Gallery, Missouri Southern State University Gallery (Solo exhibition), The Museum of Contemporary Art Georgia (Group Exhibitions), Robert and Elaine Stein Galleries, Wright State University (Group Exhibition), Dayton, Ohio, Morehead State University (Group Exhibition), Morehead, Kentucky and The Cancer Institute, St. Joseph Medical Center (Group Exhibition), Baltimore, Maryland, Orange County Center for Contemporary Art (Group Exhibition), Santa Ana, California.

"Men After Work", an oil painting by V. Rinehart & K. Kroeppler, is being exhibited at LaGrange College (GA), Lamar Dodd Art Center, for their 2024, Fifth LaGrange Southeast Regional Juried Exhibition.

Kroeppler's work has been included in the collections at Missouri Southern State University and The Kinsey Institute for Research in Sex, Gender and Reproduction. Karl Kroeppler was the Visiting Lecturer (2020-2022) at Georgia State University's Ernest G. Welch School of Art and Design.
